title: Definition.

_Let $\l\langle X,\mc{T}\r\rangle$ be a topological space. A subset $C\subseteq X$ is said to be a **closed set of $X$ w.r.t. $\mc{T}$** if $X\comp C$ is open in $\mc{T}$._
