``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $K$ be a field and fix $m,n\in\N$. Then a matrix $A\in\mat{m\times n}{K}$ is said to be of **full-rank** if_
$$\begin{equation}
    \rank\l(A\r)=\min\l\{n,m\r\}.
\end{equation}$$
```
