title: Proposition.

_Let $\l\langle K,+,\cdot\r\rangle$ be a field and fix $n\in\N^\ast$. Then the $n$-tuple space $\l\langle K^n,K,\oplus,\odot,\v{0},\v{1}\r\rangle$ over $K$ is a vector space._
