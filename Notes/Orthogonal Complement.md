``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $V$ be an inner product space. For all subsets $U\subseteq V$, the **orthogonal complement of $U$ in $V$** is the linear subspace $U^\perp\coloneqq\l\{v\in V\mid\fa u\in U:v\perp u\r\}$ of $V$.

```
