``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $V$ and $W$ be vector spaces over some field $K$ and consider a linear map $T:V\to W$. The **rank of $T$** is the dimension of $\in T$; that is,_
$$\begin{equation}
    \rank T\coloneqq\dim\im T.
\end{equation}$$

```
