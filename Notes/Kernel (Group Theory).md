title: Definition.

_Let $G$ and $H$ be groups and consider a homomorphism $\phi:G\to H$. The **kernel of $\phi$** is the subgroup_
$$\begin{equation}
    \ker\phi\coloneqq\l\{g\in G\mid\phi\l(g\r)=e\r\}=\preim_\phi\!\l\{e\r\}.
\end{equation}$$
