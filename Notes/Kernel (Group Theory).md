``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $G$ and $H$ be groups and consider a homomorphism $\phi:G\to H$. The **kernel of $\phi$** is the normal subgroup $\ker\phi\coloneqq\preim_\phi\!\l\{e\r\}=\l\{g\in G\mid\phi\l(g\r)=e\r\}$ of $G$.

```
