``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $K$ be a field and fix $A\in\mat{m\times n}{K}$ for some fixed $m,n\in\N$. The **rank of $A$** is, equivalently, $\rank\l(A\r)\coloneqq\dim\rowsp\l(A\r)$ or $\rank\l(A\r)\coloneqq\dim\colsp\l(A\r)$._

```
