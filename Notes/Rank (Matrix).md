``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $K$ be a field and fix $\v{M}\in\mat{m\times n}{K}$ for some fixed $m,n\in\N$. The **rank of $\v{M}$** is, equivalently, $\rank\l(\v{A}\r)\coloneqq\dim\rowsp\l(\v{A}\r)$ or $\rank\l(\v{A}\r)\coloneqq\dim\colsp\l(\v{A}\r)$._

```
