``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $\l\langle X,\leq\r\rangle$ be a poset and fix $S\subseteq X$. An **upper bound of $S$ in $X$** is an element $u\in X$ such that $\fa s\in S:s\leq u$. _
```
