``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $V$ be a vector space over some field $K$ and consider a linear subspace $U\subseteq V$. A **(direct) complement of $U$ in $V$** is a linear subspace $W\subseteq V$ such that $V=U\oplus W$._

```
