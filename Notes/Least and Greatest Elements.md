``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $\tpl{X,\leq}$ be a poset and fix $S\subseteq X$. The <b>least</b> (resp. <b>greatest</b>) <b>element of $S$</b> is an element $l\in S$ (resp. $g\in S$) such that
$$\begin{equation}
    \fa s\in S:l\leq s\ \ \ \ \ \ \ \ \textrm{\it{(resp. }}\fa s\in S:s\leq g\textrm{\it{)}}.
\end{equation}$$
```
