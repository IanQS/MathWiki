``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $K$ denote either $\R$ or $\C$ and let $V$ be an inner product space over $K$. The **norm induced by $\bilform{\slot}{\slot}$** is the norm_
$$\begin{equation}
    \|\slot\|:V\to\R\ \ \ \ \ \ \ \ \textrm{\it{mapping}}\ \ \ \ \ \ \ \ v\mapsto\sqrt{\bilform{v}{v}}.
\end{equation}$$
```
