title: Definition.

_Let $\l\langle X,<\r\rangle$ be a poset. A subset $Y\subseteq X$ is called an **initial segment of $X$ w.r.t $<$** if_
$$\begin{equation}
    \fa y\in Y,\fa x\in X:x<y\Rightarrow x\in Y.
\end{equation}$$
