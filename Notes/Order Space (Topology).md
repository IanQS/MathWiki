``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $\l\langle X,<\r\rangle$ be a totally ordered set. The **(topological) order space on $X$ w.r.t. $<$** is the pair $\l\langle X,\mc{T}\l(<\r)\r\rangle$ where $\mc{T}\l(<\r)$ is the order topology induced by $<$._
```
