``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $\tpl{X,d}$ be a metric space and fix $S\subseteq X$. Then **$S$ is bounded in $X$ w.r.t. $d$** if_
$$\begin{equation}
    \ex r>0,\fa x,y\in S:d\l(x,y\r)<r.
\end{equation}$$
```
