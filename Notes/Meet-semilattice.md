title: Definition.

_A poset $\l\langle X,\leq\r\rangle$ is called a **meet-semilattice** if_
$$\begin{equation}
    \fa x,y\in X,\ex l\in X:l=\inf\l\{x,y\r\}\eqqcolon x\wedge y.
\end{equation}$$
