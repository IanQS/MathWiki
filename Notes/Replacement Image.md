``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $v$ be a set and let $\phi\l(x,y\r)$ be a formula such that $\fa x\in v,\ex!y:\phi\l(x,y\r)$. The **replacement image of $v$ under $\phi\l(x,y\r)$** is the set_
$$\begin{equation}
    \im_\phi\!\l(v\r)\coloneqq\l\{y\mid\ex x\in v:\phi\l(x,y\r)\r\}.
\end{equation}$$
```
