``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $K$ be a field and take $A\in\mat{m\times n}{K}$ for some fixed $m,n\in\N$. A matrix $B\in\mat{n\times m}{K}$ is said to be a **right inverse of $A$** if $AB=I_m$._

```
