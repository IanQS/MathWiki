``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $R$ be an integral domain and fix $a\in R$ that is non-zero and is not a unit. We say that $a$ is **irreducible in $R$** if for all $r,s\in R$, $a=rs$ implies that either $r$ or $s$ is a unit in $R$._
```
