``` ad-Proposition
title: Proposition.

_Let $K$ be a field and fix $m,n\in\N$. Then, for every $A\in\mat{m\times n}{K}$, the column space $\col\l(A\r)$ is a $K$-linear subspace of $K^m$._

```
