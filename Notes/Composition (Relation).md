title: Definition.

Let $R$ and $S$ be binary relations. The <b>composition of $S$ after $R$</b> is the relation
$$\begin{equation}
    S\circ R\coloneqq\l\{\tpl{x,z}\st\ex y\l(xRy\land ySz\r)\r\}.
\end{equation}$$
