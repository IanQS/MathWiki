title: Definition.

_Let $\tpl{R,+,\cdot,0,1}$ be a ring. An element $u\in R$ is said to be a **unit in $R$** if_
$$\begin{equation}
    \ex s\in R:r\cdot s=1=s\cdot r.
\end{equation}$$
