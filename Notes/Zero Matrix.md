``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $K$ be a field and fix $m,n\in\N^\ast$. The **$m\times n$ zero matrix over $K$** is the matrix_
$$\begin{equation}
    0_{mn}\coloneqq
        \begin{bmatrix}
            0_K & \cdots & 0_K \\
            \vdots & & \vdots \\
            0_K & \cdots & 0_K
        \end{bmatrix}.
\end{equation}$$
```
