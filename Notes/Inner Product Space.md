title: Definition.

_Let $K$ denote either $\R$ or $\C$  and let $V$ be a vector space over $K$. Then $V$, equipped with an inner product $\bilform{\slot}{\slot}:V\times V\to K$, is said to be an **inner product space over $K$**._
