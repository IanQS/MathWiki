``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $f:X\to Y$. The **direct image function under $f$** is the function_
$$\begin{equation}
    \im_f:\pow\l(X\r)\to\pow\l(Y\r)\ \ \ \ \textit{mapping}\ \ \ \ S\mapsto\im_f\l(S\r)
\end{equation}$$
_where $\im_f\l(S\r)$ is the image of $S$ under $f$._
```
