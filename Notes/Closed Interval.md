``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $\tpl{X,<}$ be a totally ordered set. A **closed interval in $X$ w.r.t. $<$** is a set of the form
$$\begin{equation}
    \l[a,b\r]\coloneqq\l\{x\in X\mid\ex a,b\in X:a\leq x\leq b\r\}.
\end{equation}$$
```
