``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $V$ and $W$ be $K$-vector spaces and consider a linear map $T:V\to W$. The **image of $T$** is the linear subspace $\im T$ of $W$ and the **rank of $T$** is
$$\begin{equation}
    \rank T\coloneqq\dim\im T.
\end{equation}$$

```
