``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $G$ be a group. The **commutator subgroup of $G$** is the subgroup $\l[G,G\r]\coloneqq\gen{ghg^{-1}h^{-1}\mid g,h\in G}$._
* _An element $\l[g,h\r]\coloneqq ghg^{-1}h^{-1}\in\l[G,G\r]$ is called a **commutator**._

```
