``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $G$ be a group. The <b>commutator subgroup of $G$</b> is the subgroup $\l[G,G\r]\coloneqq\gen{ghg^{-1}h^{-1}\st g,h\in G}$.
* An element $\l[g,h\r]\coloneqq ghg^{-1}h^{-1}\in\l[G,G\r]$ is called a <b>commutator</b>.

```
