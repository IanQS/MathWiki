``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $X$ be a topological space and fix $x_0\in X$. The **fundamental group of $X$ based at $x_0$** is the group $\pi_1\l(X,x_0\r)$ of homotopy classes of loops at $x_0$ under concatenation._
```
