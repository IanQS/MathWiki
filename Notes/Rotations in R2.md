title: Definition.

_Consider the real $2$-coordinate space $\R^2$ and fix $\theta\in\R$. The **rotation transformation by $\theta$ radians counterclockwise** is the linear map_
$$\begin{equation}
    \begin{aligned}
        \rotation\l(\theta\r):\R^2&\to\R^2 \\
        \begin{bmatrix}
            x_1 \\
            x_2
        \end{bmatrix}&\mapsto
        \begin{bmatrix}
            \cos\theta & -\sin\theta \\
            \sin\theta & \cos\theta
        \end{bmatrix}
        \begin{bmatrix}
            x_1 \\
            x_2
        \end{bmatrix}.
    \end{aligned}
\end{equation}$$
