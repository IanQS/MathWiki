``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $K$ denote either $\R$ or $\C$ and let $V$ be an inner product space over $K$. A basis_ $\mc{B}\coloneqq\l\{e_i\r\}_{i\in I}$ _of $V$ is said to be an **orthonormal basis for $V$** if $\mc{B}$ is an orthonormal set._

```
