``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $\l\langle X,\leq\r\rangle$ be a poset and fix $S\subseteq X$. A **lower bound of $S$ in $X$** is an element $l\in X$ such that $\fa s\in S:l\leq s$. _
```
