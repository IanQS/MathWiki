``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $R$ and $S$ be rings and consider a homomorphism $\phi:R\to S$. The **kernel of $\phi$** is the ideal
$$\begin{equation}
    \ker\phi\coloneqq\preim_\phi\!\l\{0\r\}=\l\{r\in R\mid\phi\l(r\r)=0\r\}
\end{equation}$$
of $R$.
```
