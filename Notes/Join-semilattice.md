title: Definition.

_A poset $\tpl{X,\leq}$ is called a **join-semilattice** if_
$$\begin{equation}
    \fa x,y\in X,\ex u\in X:u=\sup\l\{x,y\r\}\eqqcolon x\vee y.
\end{equation}$$
