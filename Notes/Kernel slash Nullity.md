``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $V$ and $W$ be $K$-vector spaces and consider a linear map $T:V\to W$. The **kernel of $T$** is the linear subspace $\ker T$ of $V$ and the **nullity of $T$** is_
$$\begin{equation}
    \nullity T\coloneqq\dim\ker T.
\end{equation}$$

```
