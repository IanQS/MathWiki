``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_A ring $\tpl{R,+,\cdot,0}$ is said to be an **integral domain** if it is commutative and_
$$\begin{equation}
    \fa r,s\in R:\l[rs=0\Rightarrow\l(r=0\lor s=0\r)\r].
\end{equation}$$
```
