``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $K$ be a field and consider a vector space $V$ over $K$. The **dimension of $V$** is $\dim V\coloneqq\l|\mc{B}\r|$ where $\mc{B}\subseteq V$ is any basis for $V$._

```
