``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

A category $\cat{C}$ is said to be **thin** if for all $X,Y\in\Obj\l(\cat{C}\r)$, the hom-set $\Hom\l(X,Y\r)$ is at most a singleton.

```

<b>Remark.</b> Thin categories are locally-small.<span style="float:right;">$\blacklozenge$</span>
