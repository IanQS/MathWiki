``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $K$ be a field and consider a vector space $V$ over $K$. A subset $\mc{B}\subseteq V$ is said to be a **basis of $V$** if $\mc{B}$ is a linearly independent spanning set of $V$._

```
