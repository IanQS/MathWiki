``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $K$ denote either $\R$ or $\C$ and let $V$ be an inner product space over $K$. Two vector $v,w\in V$ are said to be **orthogonal** if $\bilform{v}{w}=0$, in which case we write $v\perp w$._

```
