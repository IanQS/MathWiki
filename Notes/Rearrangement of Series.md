``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $\tpl{a_k}$ be a sequence in $\R$ or $\C$ and fix a bijection $\sigma:\N\to\N$. The_ **_$\sigma$-rearrangement of_ $\sum_{k=0}^{\infty}a_k$** _is the infinite series_ $\sum_{k=0}^{\infty}a_{\sigma\l(k\r)}$_._

```
