title: Definition.

_Let $V$ and $W$ be vector spaces over some field $K$ and consider a linear map $T:V\to W$. The **kernel of $T$** is the set_
$$\begin{equation}
    \ker T\coloneqq\l\{v\in V\mid T\l(v\r)=0\r\}.
\end{equation}$$
