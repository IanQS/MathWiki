``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $X$ be a set. The **symmetric group on $X$** is the automorphism group $\Sym\l(X\r)\coloneqq\Aut\l(X\r)$. A subgroup of $\Sym\l(X\r)$ is called a **permutation group on $X$**._

```

**Remark.** If $X=\l\{1,\dots,n\r\}$, as is usually the case, we denote $S_n\coloneqq\Sym\l(\l\{1,\dots,n\r\}\r)$.<span style="float:right;">$\blacklozenge$</span>
