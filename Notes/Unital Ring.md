``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_A ring $\tpl{R,+,\cdot}$ is said to be a **unital ring** if it has a multiplicative identity; that is, if_
$$\begin{equation}
    \ex1\in R,\fa r\in R:1\cdot r=r=r\cdot1.
\end{equation}$$
```
