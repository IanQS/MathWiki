``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $V,W,Z$ be vector spaces over some field $K$ and consider linear maps $T:V\to W$ and $U:W\to Z$. The **composition of $U$ after $T$** is the linear map_
$$\begin{equation}
    U\circ T:V\to Z\ \ \ \ \textrm{\it{mapping}}\ \ \ \ v\mapsto U\l(T\l(v\r)\r).
\end{equation}$$
```
