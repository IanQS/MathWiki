``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $X\coloneqq\l\{1,\dots,n\r\}$. The **alternating group of degree $n$** is the permutation group $A_n\coloneqq\ker\epsilon$ where $\epsilon:S_n\to\l\{\pm1\r\}$ is the sign homomorphism._
```
