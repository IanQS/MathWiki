title: Definition.

_Let $R$ be an integral domain. We say that $R$ is a **principal ideal domain** if every ideal is principal; that is,_
$$\begin{equation}
    \fa\textrm{ \it{ideals} }I\subseteq R,\ex a\in R:I=\l\langle a\r\rangle.
\end{equation}$$
