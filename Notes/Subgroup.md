``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $\tpl{G,\ast}$ be a group and fix a subset $H\subseteq G$. The pair_ $\tpl{H,\ast_{H\times H}}$ _is a **subgroup of $\tpl{G,\ast}$** if it is itself a group._
```
