``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $R$ be a binary relation and let $S$ be a set. The **restriction of $R$ to $S$** is the binary relation
$$\begin{equation}
    R\rest S\coloneqq\l\{u\in R\mid\ex x,y:u=\tpl{x,y}\land x\in S\r\}.
\end{equation}$$
```
