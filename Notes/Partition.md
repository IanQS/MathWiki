``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $X$ be a set. A **partition of $X$** is a set $\Pi\subseteq\pow\l(X\r)$ such that:
* (Non-empty)$\bf{.}$ $\fa P\in\Pi:P\neq\em$.
* (Disjoint)$\bf{.}$ $\fa P,Q\in\Pi:P\neq Q\Rightarrow P\cap Q=\em$.
* (Cover)$\bf{.}$ $X\subseteq\bigcup\Pi$.

```
