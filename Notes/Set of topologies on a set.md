title: Definition.

_Let $X$ be a set. The **set of topologies on $X$** is the set_
$$\begin{equation}
    \Sigma\coloneqq\l\{\mc{T}\in\pow\l(\pow\l(X\r)\r)\mid\mc{T}\textrm{ is a topology on }X\r\}.
\end{equation}$$
