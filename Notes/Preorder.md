``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $X$ be a set. A binary relation $\leq$ on $X$ is a **preorder on $X$** if $\leq$ is reflexive and transitive on $X$.

```

<b>Remark.</b> Write $\geq\,\coloneqq\,\leq^{-1}$.<span style="float:right;">$\blacklozenge$</span>
