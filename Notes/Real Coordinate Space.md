title: Definition.

_Consider the field of real numbers $\l\langle\R,+,\cdot\r\rangle$ and fix $n\in\N^\ast$. The **real $n$-coordinate space** is the $n$-tuple space $\l\langle\R^n,\R,\oplus,\odot,\v{0}\r\rangle$._
