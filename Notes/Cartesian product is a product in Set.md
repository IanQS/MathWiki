``` ad-Proposition
title: Proposition.

_Let_ $\l\{X_i\r\}_{i\in I}$ _be an indexed family of sets. Then_ $\prod_{i\in I}X_i$_, together with the projection maps_ $\pi_j:\prod_{i\in I}X_i\to X_j$_, is a product in $\catset$._

```

_Proof_.
