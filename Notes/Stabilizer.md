``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $G$ be a group acting on a set $X$ and fix $x\in X$. The **stabilizer/isotropy subgroup of $x$ under $G$** is the subgroup $G_x\coloneqq\l\{g\in G\mid gx=x\r\}$._

```
