``` ad-Proposition
title: Proposition.

_Let $K$ denote either $\R$ or $\C$ and let $V$ be a finite-dimensional inner product space over $K$. If $U\subseteq V$ is a linear subspace, then_
$$\begin{equation}
    V=U\oplus U^\perp.
\end{equation}$$

```

_Proof_.
