``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $V$ and $W$ be vector spaces over some field $K$. A **$K$-linear isomorphism from $V$ to $W$** is an invertible linear map $T:V\to W$. That is, linear isomorphisms are isomorphisms in $\catvect[K]$._
```
