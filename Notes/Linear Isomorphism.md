``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $K$ be a field. Two vector spaces $V$ and $W$ over $K$ are said to be **linearly isomorphic** if there exists an invertible linear map $T:V\to W$, in which case $T$ is said to be a **linear isomorphism**._

```
