``` ad-Proposition
title: Proposition.

_Let $K$ be a field and fix $m,n\in\N$. Then, for every linear map $T:K^n\to K^m$, there exists a unique matrix $A\in\mat{m\times n}{K}$ such that $T\l(\v{x}\r)=A\v{x}$ for all $\v{x}\in K^n$._

```
