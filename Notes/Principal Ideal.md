``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $R$ be a commutative ring and fix $a\in R$. The <b>principal ideal generated by $a$ in $R$</b> is the ideal
$$\begin{equation}
    \sring{a}\coloneqq\l\{ar\in R\mid r\in R\r\}.
\end{equation}$$
```
