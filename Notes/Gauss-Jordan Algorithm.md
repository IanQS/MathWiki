``` ad-Proposition
title: Proposition.

_Let $K$ be a field and fix $m,n\in\N$. Then every matrix $\v{M}\in\mat{m\times n}{K}$ is row-equivalent to a matrix $\v{R}\in\mat{m\times n}{K}$ in reduced row-echelon form._
```
