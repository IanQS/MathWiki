``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $V$ and $W$ be $K$-vector spaces and consider a linear map $T:V\to W$. The <b>kernel of $T$</b> is the linear subspace $\ker T$ of $V$ and the <b>nullity of $T$</b> is
$$\begin{equation}
    \nullity T\coloneqq\dim\ker T.
\end{equation}$$

```
