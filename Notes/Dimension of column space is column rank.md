title: Proposition.

_Let $K$ be a field and consider a matrix $A\in\mat{m\times n}{K}$ for some fixed $m,n\in\N$. Then_
$$\begin{equation}
    \dim\col\l(A\r)=\col\rank\l(A\r).
\end{equation}$$
