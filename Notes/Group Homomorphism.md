``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $\tpl{G,\ast_G}$ and $\tpl{H,\ast_H}$ be groups. A **group homomorphism from $G$ to $H$** is a function $\phi:G\to H$ such that_
$$\begin{equation}
    \fa g_1,g_2\in G:\phi\l(g_1\ast_Gg_2\r)=\phi\l(g_1\r)\ast_H\phi\l(g_2\r).
\end{equation}$$
```
