``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $\cat{C}$ be a category and fix $X\in\Obj\l(\cat{C}\r)$. An **automorphism of $X$** is an endomorphism of $X$ that is also an isomorphism. The set of all automorphisms forms a group $\Aut\l(X\r)$ under composition.

```
