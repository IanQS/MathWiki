title: Definition.

_Let $X\overset{f}{\rightarrow}Y\overset{p}{\leftarrow}E$ be continuous maps between topological spaces. A map $\tilde{f}:X\to E$ is said to be a **lifting of $f$ w.r.t. $p$** if $p\circ\tilde{f}=f$._
