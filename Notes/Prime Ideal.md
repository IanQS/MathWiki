``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $I\subset R$ be a proper ideal of a commutative ring $R$. Then $I$ is said to be a **prime ideal in $R$** if
$$\begin{equation}
    \fa r,s\in R:rs\in I\Rightarrow r\in I\lor s\in I.
\end{equation}$$
```
