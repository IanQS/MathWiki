``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

A proper ideal $\mf{p}\subset R$ is said to be <b>prime</b> if $rs\in\mf{p}$ implies either $r\in\mf{p}$ or $s\in\mf{p}$ for all $r,s\in R$.
```
