!!! ad-Definition Definition.

_Let $\l\langle X,\mc{T}\r\rangle$ be a topological space and fix a subset $Y\subseteq X$. The pair $\l\langle Y,\l.\mc{T}\r|_Y\r\rangle$, where $\l.\mc{T}\r|_Y$ is the subset topology on $Y$ inherited from $X$, is called a **(topological) subspace of $\l\langle X,\mc{T}\r\rangle$**._
