!!! ad-Definition Definition.

Let $f:X\to Y$ and $g:Y\to Z$. The **composition function of $g$ after $f$** is the function
$$\begin{equation}
    \begin{aligned}
        g\circ f:X&\to Z\\
        x&\mapsto g\l(f\l(x\r)\r).
    \end{aligned}
\end{equation}$$
