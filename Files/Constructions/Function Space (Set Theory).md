!!! ad-Definition Definition.

Let $X$ and $Y$ be sets. The **function space of $X$ into $Y$** is the set
$$\begin{equation}
    Y^X\coloneqq\l\{\Gamma_{\mathclap{f}}\;\in\ms{P}\l(X\times Y\r)\mid\l(\Gamma_{\mathclap{f}}\;,X,Y\r)\mathrm{\ is\ a\ function}\r\}.
\end{equation}$$
