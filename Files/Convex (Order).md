title: Definition.

_Let $\l\langle X,<\r\rangle$ be a totally ordered set. A subset $Y\subseteq X$ is said to be **convex (in $X$)** if_
$$\begin{equation}
    \fa a,b\in Y:a<b\Rightarrow\l(a,b\r)_X\subseteq Y.
\end{equation}$$
