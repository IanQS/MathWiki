!!! ad-Definition Definition (Ordered Pair).

Let $x$ and $y$ be sets. An **ordered pair of $x$ and $y$** is the set $\l\langle x,y\r\rangle\coloneqq\l\{\l\{x\r\},\l\{x,y\r\}\r\}$.
