``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_A poset $\l\langle X,\leq\r\rangle$ is said to be **bounded** if there exist $g,l\in X$ such that $\fa x\in X:l\leq x\leq g$, that is, if $X$ has both greatest and least elements._

```
