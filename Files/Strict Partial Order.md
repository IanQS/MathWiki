!!! ad-Definition Definition.

Let $X$ be a set. A binary relation $<$ is a **strict partial order on $X$** if $<$ is transitive and irreflexive on $X$.

--- admonition

**Remark.** Write $>\,\coloneqq\,<^{-1}$.<span style="float:right;">$\blacklozenge$</span>
