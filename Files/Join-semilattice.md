!!! ad-Definition Definition.

A poset $\l\langle X,\leq\r\rangle$ is called a **join-semilattice** if
$$\begin{equation}
    \fa x,y\in X,\ex u\in X:u=\sup\l\{x,y\r\}\eqqcolon x\vee y.
\end{equation}$$
