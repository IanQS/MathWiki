!!! ad-Definition Definition.

Let $X$ be a set and let $\Pi$ be a partition on $X$. The **relation induced by $\Pi$** is the relation $R_\Pi$ defined by
$$\begin{equation}
    xR_\Pi y\ \ \ \ \Leftrightarrow\ \ \ \ \ex P\in\Pi:x\in P\land y\in P
\end{equation}$$
for all $x,y\in X$.
