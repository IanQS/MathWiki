!!! ad-Definition Definition.

Let $X$ be a set and let $<$ be a well-ordering on $X$. A subset $Y\subseteq X$ is called an **initial segment of $X$ w.r.t $<$** if
$$\begin{equation}
    \fa y\in Y,\fa x\in X:x<y\Rightarrow x\in Y.
\end{equation}$$
