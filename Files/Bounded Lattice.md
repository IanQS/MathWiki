``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_A lattice $\l\langle X,\leq\r\rangle$ is said to be **bounded** if there exist $\top,\bot\in X$ such that $\fa x\in X:\bot\leq x\leq\top$, that is, if its underlying poset is bounded._
```
