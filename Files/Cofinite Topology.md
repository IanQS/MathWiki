``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $X$ be a set. The **cofinite topology on $X$** is the topology_
$$\begin{equation}
    \mc{T}_\textit{cf}\coloneqq\l\{U\in\mc{P}\l(X\r)\mid\l(U=\em\r)\lor\l(X\comp U\textit{ is finite}\r)\r\}.
\end{equation}$$
```
