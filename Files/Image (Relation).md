!!! ad-Definition Definition.

Let $R$ be a binary relation and let $S$ be a set. The **image of $S$ under $R$** is the set
$$\begin{equation}
    \im_R\l(S\r)\coloneqq R\l[x\r]\coloneqq\ran\l(R\rest S\r).
\end{equation}$$
