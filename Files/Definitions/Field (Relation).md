!!! ad-Definition Definition.

Let $\mc{R}\subseteq X\times Y$. The **field of $\mc{R}$** is the set
$$\begin{equation}
    \fld\mc{R}\coloneqq\dom\mc{R}\cup\ran\mc{R}.
\end{equation}$$
