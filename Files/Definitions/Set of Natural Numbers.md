!!! ad-Definition Definition.

_Let $I$ be any inductive set asserted by the Axiom of Infinity. The **set of natural numbers** is the set_
$$\begin{equation}
    \omega\coloneqq\l\{n\in I\mid n\textit{ is a natural number}\r\}.
\end{equation}$$
