!!! ad-Definition Definition.

Let $f:X\to Y$ be a function and fix a subset $S\subseteq X$. The **restriction of $f$ to $S$** is the function
$$\begin{equation}
    \begin{aligned}
        \l.f\r|_S:S&\to Y\\
        x&\mapsto f\l(x\r).
    \end{aligned}
\end{equation}$$
