!!! ad-Definition Definition.

Let $\mc{R}\subseteq X\times Y$. The **domain of $\mc{R}$** is the set
$$\begin{equation}
    \dom\mc{R}\coloneqq\l\{x\in X\mid\l(\ex y\in Y\r)x\mc{R}y\r\}.
\end{equation}$$
