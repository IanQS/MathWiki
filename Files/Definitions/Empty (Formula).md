!!! ad-Definition Definition.

Consider the first-order logic with a set of non-logical symbols $\mc{L}=\l\{\in\r\}$. Then **$\operatorname{emp}\l(x\r)$** is the $\mc{L}$-formula $\fa z\l(z\not\in x\r)$.
