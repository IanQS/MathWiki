!!! ad-Axiom Axiom.

Consider the first-order logic with a set of non-logical symbols $\mc{L}=\l\{\in\r\}$. The **Axiom of Pairing** is the universal closure of the $\mc{L}$-formula
$$\begin{equation}
    \ex z\l(x\in z\land y\in z\r).
\end{equation}$$
