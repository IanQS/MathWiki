!!! ad-Definition Definition.

_Let $\l\langle X,<\r\rangle$ be a totally ordered set. A **closed interval in $X$ w.r.t $<$** is a set of the form_
$$\begin{equation}
    \l[a,b\r]\coloneqq\l\{x\in X\mid\ex a,b\in X:a\leq x\leq b\r\}.
\end{equation}$$
