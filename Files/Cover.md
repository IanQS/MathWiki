!!! ad-Definition Definition.

Let $X$ be a set. A set $\mc{C}$ is called a **cover of $X$** if $X\subseteq\bigcup\mc{C}$. That is, if
$$\begin{equation}
    \fa x\in X,\ex C\in\mc{C}:x\in C.
\end{equation}$$
