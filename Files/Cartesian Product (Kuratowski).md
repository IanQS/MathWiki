!!! ad-Definition Definition.

Let $X$ and $Y$ be sets. The **Cartesian product of $X$ and $Y$** is the set
$$\begin{equation}
    X\times Y\coloneqq\l\{u\mid\ex x\in X,\ex y\in Y:u=\l\langle x,y\r\rangle\r\}.
\end{equation}$$
