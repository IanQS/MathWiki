!!! ad-Definition Definition.

Let $X$ be a set. A binary relation $R$ is **antisymmetric on $X$** if
$$\begin{equation}
    \fa x,y\in X:\l(xRy\land yRx\r)\Rightarrow x=y.
\end{equation}$$
