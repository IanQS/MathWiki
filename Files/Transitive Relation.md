!!! ad-Definition Definition.

_Let $X$ be a set. A binary relation $R$ is **transitive on $X$** if_
$$\begin{equation}
    \fa x,y,z\in X:\l(xRy\land yRz\r)\Rightarrow xRz.
\end{equation}$$
